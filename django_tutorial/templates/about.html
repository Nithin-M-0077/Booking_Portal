ABOUT US

Hope & Care Hospital


Being the consistent leading tertiary care service provider in the Northern part of Kerala, for the past 33 years, we are committed to excelling in cutting-edge technologies to compassionate patient care and International standard of treatment protocols and outstanding outcomes. We have introduced various healthcare technologies in our center and have a dedicated team of internationally qualified doctors specializing in various fields of healthcare. Our well-equipped infrastructure emphasizes on patient safety, satisfaction and outcomes. Our full range of primary specialty care medical services enables cross-specialty consultation, which assures outstanding treatment for each patient. This high-tech infrastructure over 3.5Lakhs sqft of built-up area spread over 9 acres of land. The service of the institution is nationally accredited by the National accreditation board for hospitals and healthcare providers [NABH]. With extensive facilities comprising 20 super specialty departments, 11 most modern operation theatres, a large intensive care unit, a fully computerized and networked hospital information system (HIS). a fully digital radiology department, ultra-modern clinical laboratories and 24x7 emergency department with a mobile ICU.Hope & Care hospital is strategically located in the heart of American city- and easily accessible by air, road and rail. We have a special cell for international patients relations and functions exclusively to take care of their needs. A host of facilities from airport pick-up to priority appointments with specialties are provided to make way for smooth treatment period.
